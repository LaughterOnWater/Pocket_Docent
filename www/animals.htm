  Turtles

Turtles come in three flavors. Tortoises spend most of their time on land. Terrapins live only in brackish marshes and coastal inlets. The rest of the turtles, marine or otherwise, spend most of their time in water. All tortoises and terrapins are also turtles.

  • Yellow-bellied Slider
