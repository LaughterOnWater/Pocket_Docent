Reptile House

The Reptile House is the largest building at the center of the Zoo. The front right window of the Reptile House shows our Eastern Indigo Snake. Around the corner to the left is the entrance to the Reptile House. It is open Saturdays from 1 to 4pm, and some Sundays, depending on volunteer scheduling. It is occasionally opened for additional planned events. There are also various amphibians. arthropods and sometimes mammals viewable at the Reptile House.

Entrance

The Main Entrance to the Zoo is a black iron gate between two stone walls. On either side of the Main Entrance are signage with event information and rules about what can happen at the zoo. Our animals are happiest when their visitors follow these rules!

Amphitheatre

The Amphitheatre seats about eighty people comfortably, with room for more to stand at the sides and up toward the barn. Musical artists often grace this venue during special events like Zoo Day. The Amphitheatre is close to the barn, a storage place for tools and zoo vehicles.

Education Center

The Education Center is where volunteers get trained. It's also where we prepare meals for our animals and where volunteers sign in and out. Zoo birthday parties are held here almost every Saturday and Sunday, except during Zoo Camp months in the summer.

Exercise Yard

The Exercise Yard is where we bring our animals for enrichment, excercise and a little sun. It's also a wonderful space for educating our visitors close up about our incredible native Georgia animals. It lies between the Bear/Eagle Deck and the Reptile House.

Playground

Bear Hollow Zoo is a special part of Memorial Park. The Playground is outside the zoo at the extreme opposite end of Memorial Park. You can see it from the overflow parking lot to the left as you drive or walk down toward the Memorial Park pond. Additional restrooms are immediately adjacent to the Playground.

Restrooms

The restroom is outside the Zoo at the front of Memorial Park's public pool. There is also a restroom to the left of the large parking lot near the playground. Click the "map" button to the bottom right for more information.
